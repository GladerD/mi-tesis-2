\chapter{Marco Teorico}

Las ecuaciones de Maxwell-Heaviside se muestran en \eqref{eq:maxwell-gauss}--\eqref{eq:maxwell-ampere}. \cite{heaviside}:

\begin{gather}
    \nabla \cdot \mathbf{E} = \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0} \label{eq:maxwell-gauss} \\
    \nabla \cdot \mathbf{B} = 0 \label{eq:maxwell-gaussmag} \\
    \nabla \times \mathbf{E} = -\frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t} \label{eq:maxwell-faraday} \\
    \nabla \times \mathbf{B} = \mu \mathbf{J} + \mu \varepsilon \frac{\partial \mathbf{E}}{\partial t} \label{eq:maxwell-ampere}
\end{gather}
